## Mavericks Trackpad 2 — v0.4.2

Fixes the About tab intermittently disappearing.

- **The About tab now shows reliably** — under some conditions the pane's About tab could fail to
  appear, and stay gone across quitting and reopening System Preferences, until it recovered on its
  own. It now appears consistently.

Requires macOS 10.9.5 or later (the 10.9 series).
